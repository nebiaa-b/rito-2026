About Me

Kia ora. I’m Nebiaa, a Year 12 student at Onslow College in Wellington. I’m passionate about helping young people feel informed, confident, and connected to how our democracy works.

Video 1 – Introduction

In this video, I introduce who I am, what I’m studying, and why I’m applying for Rito o te Pāremata. I share what motivates me, and how my experiences at school have shaped my interest in helping other rangatahi engage with Parliament.

Video 2 – Biggest Barrier

Here, I explain what I believe is the biggest barrier for young people engaging with Parliament — the feeling of being disconnected or unsure about where to start. I talk about why this matters and how clearer youth-friendly communication can make participation easier.

Video 3 – Things I Improved

In this section, I reflect on the personal improvements I have made, including confidence, communication, and stepping forward in leadership roles. I explain how these changes help me contribute positively to a group like Rito o te Pāremata.
